Tweep Jumper

Tweep Jumper is a Google Chrome extension for Twitter that took a day and a half from concept to launch. We decided it would be a fun project and we were itching to build something. Tweep Jumper allows you to quickly jump between your top 10 tweeps. Sometimes you just want to view someones tweets and we found no simple way to do this.

Solar Ship

Developed in partnership with Idea Bank Inc., solarship.com is the static website of the aerospace company Solar Ship Inc. We worked closely with Idea Bank's Lead Industrial Designer Andrew Leinonen to maintain Solarship's branding, while creating an aesthetic that would keep the site looking fresh with minimal content.

Beat District

Beat District (BD) is a highly dynamic urban music competition site, featuring state-of-the-art technology used to facilitate continuous music playback while browsing the site. BD got as far as public beta before we signed over the project to one of the Co-Founders. There's talk about a relaunch in the near future but Ideasylum is no longer involved.

Connectsy

Connectsy is a location-based mobile app for Android the makes it simple to share your plans and get together with friends on short notice. Connectsy officially launched at Sprout Up in November, 2010 and received a great response. Despite getting some good press, we decided to pivot Connectsy in a new direction in 2011.

Fio

Developed in partnership with Idea Bank Inc., fio.com is an informational site for Fio Corporation, a biotech company specializing in rapid diagnostics of infectious disease. Due to Fio's involvement internationally, it was especially important for their website to be available in multiple languages. We made heavy use of Django's internationalization to achieve this goal.

Mazava

Developed in partnership with Idea Bank Inc., Mazava is a web application focused on funding doctors and entrepreneurs in Africa. It features a mobile application designed for use in Africa, facilitating entrepreneurs to upload media related to their ideas. Once ideas are posted, Mazava enables funding for them by leveraging the PayPal API.
